About

Xuejun Cheng is currently pursuing an MSc in Artificial Intelligence at Monash University (Malaysia & Australia campus). His research interests span artificial intelligence, interdisciplinary applications in marketing, law, financial markets, and quantum computing. He is also a research assistant at Harbin Institute of Technology, Shenzhen / Shenzhen Loop Area Institution, and a university research assistant at Coventry University's School of Business.
